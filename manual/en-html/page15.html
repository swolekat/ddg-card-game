Calculating Scores
The first person to match the sum of their distance cards to the station card wins. Remember if you go over, that line does not count. The distance on the station is the number of points you get for winning. After playing several rounds, the player with the greatest sum of points wins.
※ You get 0 points if you do not win the round.
