How to use Guard Cards
There are two Guard cards: Go cards and Emergency Break Cards. Go cards remove Signal Interference cards and Emergency Breaks remove Emergency cards.
1) An Interference card is played on you.
2) Draw a card (because it is now your turn) and play the appropriate Guard card. If you don't have the right card, resolve the affects of the interference card and play normally.
3) If you played the Guard card, place both the Interference card and Guard card in the discard pile.
